Being mentally aware

Being mentally aware means knowing that people are susceptible to be vulnerable.
Having a mental health problem has never been uncommon, it's just that now, thanks to science and research, people become more aware of it.



Mindfulness is one of the best practices

Mindfulness is the psychological process of bringing one's attention to experiences occurring in the present moment, which one can develop through the practice of meditation and through other training. Wikipedia

Some experts believe that mindfulness works, in part, by helping people to accept their experiences—including painful emotions—rather than react to them with aversion and avoidance.
It’s become increasingly common for mindfulness meditation to be combined with psychotherapy, especially cognitive behavioral therapy. This development makes good sense, since both meditation and cognitive behavioral therapy share the common goal of helping people gain perspective on irrational, maladaptive, and self-defeating thoughts. helpguide.org
